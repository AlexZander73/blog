Launch Checklist I Reuse for Micro Products

Shipping gets dramatically easier when launch details are standardized. This checklist keeps my release days structured, calm, and less error-prone.

January 29, 20266 min read

Launches Apps Design

Before Coding Is "Done"

I define a minimum launch package early: one clear value proposition, one primary user path, one clear call to action. If those are fuzzy, launch date is fiction.

  • Landing copy draft prepared
  • Core screenshots captured
  • Error states tested on mobile and desktop

Week of Launch

This week is for clarity, not feature expansion. I run a daily checklist sweep to ensure messaging, pricing, onboarding, and support links all align.

I also prepare post-launch tracking ahead of time so learning starts immediately once real users arrive.

Launch Day and After

On launch day I focus on responsiveness, not new development. The first 48 hours are about support, bug triage, and capturing qualitative feedback while context is fresh.

Launch is not the finish line, it is the start of the feedback loop.

Reusing this checklist has reduced release stress and improved quality on every small project I ship.
