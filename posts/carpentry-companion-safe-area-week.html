Backlog Entry: Safe-Area Week and the Black-Screen Scare

Backlog entry for 2026-03-04. This was one of the rougher stretches. On-device runs exposed notch clipping and startup weirdness that desktop testing never showed.

March 4, 20265 min read

Apps Build Logs Notes

What broke in real use

The header clipped into the notch. Startup behavior occasionally looked black or blank. On paper these seem like UI bugs, but in practice they break first trust. If the first screen feels unstable, users assume the whole app is unstable.

I patched safe-area handling and corrected scene storyboard wiring. Those fixes did not add new features, but they protected the product from feeling fragile.

The emotional side

This was one of those days where progress felt invisible. I worked for hours and the app looked almost the same in screenshots. But the experience felt very different on device.

I had to remind myself that stability work is not a detour. It is the product.

Lesson and next move

Lesson: native shell polish is part of core value when your app lives on phones.

Next move was to keep pressure on mobile UX details, especially navigation behavior, before moving deeper into release admin tasks.

Next move: stress-test navigation and interaction layers on iPhone.
